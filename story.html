The tale starts with a hero who, after having fought a battle that lasted 152 hours, sat leaning against a sturdy tree.

The hero sat and watched the town as they celebrated that night. The hero saw a great many of things. A trio of siblings in yellow apparel playing with one another. Three jesters in green making wordplay to all who will listen. And one person in bright magenta, telling tales to the passerby.

While the hero observed the people, an apple came into the corner of the hero's vision. A group of five had baskets full of apples, and they were offering them to everyone around the town. The hero thanked them and took the apple. By the time the hero was finished with the apple, only one in that group had a full basket still.

The hero, after experienced the large battle that had occured beforehand and watching the townsfolk go along thier merry business, had one thought,

.

'This peace is a thing that should be cherished'

.
